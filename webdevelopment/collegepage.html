Founded in 1957 by philanthropist and industrialist Late Shri.Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar,Thiagarajar College of Engineering(TCE) is an ISO 9001:2008 certified institution affiliated to Anna University and situated in Madurai,the Temple City.The college is funded by Central & State Governments and Management.The courses offered in TCE are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education,New Delhi.TCE was granted Autonomy in the year 1987 and the programmes have been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation(NBA).TCE offers 8 undergraduate programmes,13 postgraduate programs and doctoral programmes in Engineering,Science and Architecture.
